Foundation — Can You Recall It?

Worth 1-2 marks each. Vocabulary and basic structures.
1
Give the Spanish for: maths, science (physics), history, art, PE.
2 marks
Answer
las matemáticas, la física, la historia, el dibujo, la educación física [1 for 3+, 2 for all 5].
Accept “las ciencias” for science generally. Accents on matemáticas and física matter in writing.
Rate:
2
Translate: “I like biology because it is interesting.” Then: “I don’t like maths because it is difficult.”
2 marks
Answer
“Me gusta la biología porque es interesante.” [1]
“No me gustan las matemáticas porque son difíciles.” [1]
Second sentence must use “gustan” (plural) and “son” (plural) because matemáticas is plural. Also “difíciles” not “difícil.”

3
How do you say these times in Spanish? (a) 8:30 (b) 1:00 (c) 3:15 (d) 9:45
2 marks
Answer
(a) las ocho y media, (b) la una, (c) las tres y cuarto, (d) las diez menos cuarto [½ each].
Notice (b) uses “la una” (singular). And (d) 9:45 = “ten minus quarter” in Spanish thinking.

4
What is ESO in the Spanish school system? At what age does compulsory education end in Spain?
1 mark
Answer
ESO = Educación Secundaria Obligatoria (compulsory secondary education). Compulsory education ends at age 16 [1].

Intermediate — Can You Use It?

Worth 3-4 marks each. Sentences with opinions, time, and tense accuracy.
5
Describe your school day in Spanish (4 sentences). Include: what time you get up, when classes start, your favourite subject with a reason, and what time classes end.
4 marks
Model Answer
“Me levanto a las siete. Las clases empiezan a las ocho y media. Mi asignatura favorita es la biología porque es interesante. Las clases terminan a las tres y media.”

[1] correct time expression, [1] opinion + porque, [1] correct vocabulary (asignatura, clases), [1] overall accuracy.
This is a common IGCSE writing task. The pattern: time + activity, time + activity, opinion + reason.
Rate:
6
Put these verbs into the imperfect tense: (a) yo / estudiar, (b) ella / comer, (c) nosotros / vivir, (d) ellos / hablar, (e) tú / ser, (f) yo / ir
3 marks
Answer
(a) estudiaba, (b) comía, (c) vivíamos, (d) hablaban, (e) eras (irregular!), (f) iba (irregular!) [½ each].
The regulars follow the pattern: -AR → -aba, -ER/-IR → -ía. The irregulars (ser, ir) must be memorised.
Rate:
7
Translate into Spanish: “I prefer music because the teacher is fun. I hate chemistry because it is very difficult. In my opinion, English is the most useful subject.”
3 marks
Answer
“Prefiero la música porque el profesor es divertido.” [1]
“Odio la química porque es muy difícil.” [1]
“En mi opinión, el inglés es la asignatura más útil.” [1]
The third sentence uses a superlative (la más útil). Bonus if you get that structure right — it shows range.

Advanced — Can You Describe and Compare?

Worth 5-6 marks each. Extended writing with multiple tenses and opinions.
8
Write 5-6 sentences comparing your school in Denmark with school in Florida. Use the imperfect tense for Denmark (how things were) and the present tense for Florida (how things are now). Include at least one opinion.
6 marks
Model Answer
“En Dinamarca, mi colegio era pequeño y moderno. Estudiaba muchas asignaturas y los profesores eran simpáticos. Las clases empezaban a las ocho. Ahora vivo en Florida. Mi colegio es más grande. Me gustan las clases porque son interesantes.”

[1] imperfect tense used correctly (era, estudiaba, eran, empezaban), [1] present tense used correctly (es, vivo, gustan, son), [1] two tenses contrasted, [1] comparative (más grande), [1] opinion + reason, [1] overall accuracy.
Using two tenses in one paragraph is the single biggest mark booster for IGCSE writing. The examiner sees range and rewards it.
Rate:
9
Translate into Spanish: “When I was young, I used to go to a small school. The teachers were very nice. I used to study English and maths. Now I study more subjects and my school is bigger.”
5 marks
Answer
“Cuando era joven, iba a un colegio pequeño. Los profesores eran muy simpáticos. Estudiaba inglés y matemáticas. Ahora estudio más asignaturas y mi colegio es más grande.”

[1] era (irregular imperfect of ser), [1] iba (irregular imperfect of ir), [1] regular imperfects (estudiaba, eran), [1] present tense contrast (estudio, es), [1] comparative (más grande).
This tests both irregular imperfects (era, iba) and regular ones (estudiaba), plus a tense switch to present. Five grammar points = five marks.
Rate:
10
Write a full paragraph (5+ sentences) about your ideal school. Use opinions (me gustaría / I would like), say what subjects you would study, what the school would be like, and compare it with your current school. Use at least two tenses.
6 marks
Model Answer
“Mi colegio ideal sería grande y moderno. Me gustaría estudiar música, informática y educación física. Las clases empezarían a las nueve y no habría deberes. Los profesores serían divertidos. Mi colegio actual es bueno pero es más pequeño que mi colegio ideal.”

[1] conditional forms (sería, gustaría, empezarían), [1] subject vocabulary, [1] opinion phrase, [1] comparison with current school, [1] present tense for current school, [1] accuracy and variety.
The conditional (would) is formed by adding -ía endings to the infinitive. Even one conditional sentence shows the examiner you can handle advanced grammar.
